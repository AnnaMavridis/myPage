Introduction

Hello! And thank you for stumbling accross this site.
My name is Anna, but you have probably already seen that. This website you are visiting is actually just for my own use. To keep thing on track and make me feel accountable to do what I set my mind to.

Let me tell my story (to probably noone):
I just turned 20 years old and I have struggled with serious mental illnesses in the last decade. It got to the point where I had to quit school and lost all my friends and social life. If I'm being honest to you (again probably noone is actally ever going to read this lol) I thought for many of my teenage-years, that I would not be getting out of this alive.
But I did. I mean I am still here, right? And I am getting better. Sooo much better!

Oh yeah the point of this website..
If you have a quick look in the menu (top right) you will see my goals, accomplishments and just random stuff I think about while working through my illnes. You can also leave a message to me if you want to on the contact page (againnnn I am talking to noone).
