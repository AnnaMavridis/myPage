My Dog

I have the cutest dog ever. Like EVER! Her name is Ginny. And she's 2 years old.
I love her more than life. So here is a few cute pictures of her


We adopted her in November 2020 when my mental health was at a low. She helped me become a better person. I love her so much and she never fails to gift me a smile. She got me through many dark moments and I am the proudest dog mom I could be.
